Ice Hockey

About Hockey

Ice hockey is a team sport in which both teams try to score a rubber flat puck into the opponent's goal.
5 players from each side try to score the puck into the goal with each team having a goalie protecting them from being scored on.
Hockey is a very fast-paced and physical sport and fights are very common to breakout during the games at moments.
Hockey's main popularity is in countries such as Canada and Russia and some parts of the U.S.

The NHL

The NHL is the premier hockey league around the world. There are 31 teams in the league
with a 32nd being approved for 2021. The most recent champions of the league are the Washington Capitals.

About the Defending Champions

The Washington Capitals are the most recent champions of the NHL.
They defeated the Vegas Golden Knights 4 games to 1 in the 2018 Stanley Cup .
They are led by MVP Alexander Ovechkin and have a great group of supporting players
such as Lars Eller and T.J Oshie and Michal Kempny .
