Basketball

About Basketball

Basketball is a sport that requires teamwork and coordination on the court.
Basketball is a five-on-five sport in which the main objective is to score the basketball into the hoop.
The premier professional league for basketball is the NBA which has 30 teams all over the U.S.
The most recent NBA champions were the Golden State Warriors.

The NBA

The NBA is home to 30 teams, 15 from the Western Conference and 15 from the Eastern Conference .
There are 3 divisions in each conference with each division containing 5 teams.
8 teams from each conference make the playoffs and battle it out to win the NBA Finals.

About the Defending Champions

The Golden State Warriors recently won their 3rd championship in the last 4 years. The Warriors
are led by their all star quartet of Two Times Final MVP Kevin Durant, Two Time MVP Stephen Curry,
Sharpshooter Klay Thompson, and defensive star Draymond Green. The Warriors have made 4 straight NBA Final's appearances.
