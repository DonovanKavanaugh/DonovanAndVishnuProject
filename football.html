About Football

Football is a team sport in which one side is playing offense and the other is playing defense.
There are 11 players on both sides of the field offensively and defensively. The main objective
of the game is to get into the endzone witht the football and score 7 points. You can also
kick field goals which are worth 3 points each and get a defensive safety which is worth 2 points.

The NFL

The National Football League is composed of 32 teams across the U.S. The league is split up into two main divisions, the .
NFC and the AFC. There are 4 divisions in both sides and each winner of the division goes to the.
playoffs along with two wild card teams with the highest records. The most recent NFL champions were the Philadelphia Eagles.

About the Defending Champions

The Eagles went 13-3 last season and went into the playoffs as underdogs to the rest of the teams
due to losing their starting quarterback Carson Wentz to an ACL tear. This didn't deter the Eagles hopes and they stormed through the playoffs
and eventually won the Super Bowl with the great play of backup and Super Bowl MVP Nick Foles.
