Baseball

About Baseball

Baseball is a team sport in which one team has a lineup of people trying to hit the ball and score
while the ther team pitches the ball and tries to catch it if it is hit.
Baseball is slow-paced compared to other sports and it requires mental sharpness along with great reaction timing.
The premier baseball league is the Major Baseball League in the U.S but baseball is also very popular in South America and Japan as well.

The MLB

The MLB is the oldest major sports league in America and is home to 30 teams which compete
for a World Series trophy. The most recent champions of the MLB are the Boston Red Sox who won their 9th title in 2018.

About the Defending Champions

The Boston Red Sox were winners of the 2018 World Series.
They defeated the Los Angeles Dodgers in 5 games .
They are led by Mookie Betts, Chris Sale and Steve Pearce
